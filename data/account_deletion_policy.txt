ACCOUNT DELETION AND DATA RETENTION POLICY
==========================================

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. ACCOUNT DELETION REQUEST
----------------------------
To request permanent account deletion:
  - Go to Settings → Account → Danger Zone → Delete Account
  - Confirm with your password
  - A 14-day grace period begins immediately

During the 14-day grace period, you can cancel the deletion by logging in and
clicking "Cancel Deletion" in the account banner.

After 14 days, all account data is permanently and irreversibly removed.

2. WHAT IS DELETED
-------------------
Upon confirmed deletion, the following data is permanently removed:
  - User profile and credentials
  - All conversation threads and message history
  - API keys and integration configurations
  - Billing payment methods (not invoices — see Section 4)
  - Uploaded files and attachments

3. WHAT IS RETAINED
--------------------
The following data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate
business purposes:
  - Invoices and payment records: retained for 7 years (tax compliance)
  - Anonymized aggregate usage statistics (no personal identifiers)
  - Records required for fraud prevention or legal obligations

4. BILLING RECORDS
------------------
Invoices issued before account deletion are retained for 7 years in accordance
with financial regulations. You can download all invoices before deletion from
Billing → Invoice History.

5. DATA EXPORT BEFORE DELETION
--------------------------------
We strongly recommend exporting your data before requesting deletion:
  - Settings → Data → Export All
  - Exports are provided as a ZIP file within 24 hours
  - Export link is valid for 72 hours

6. TEAM ACCOUNTS
-----------------
Only the account Owner can delete an organization account.
All member accounts must be removed before the organization can be deleted.

7. CONTACT
----------
For account deletion questions: privacy@example.com
Response time: 1–2 business days
