# Payment Methods and Billing FAQ

## Accepted Payment Methods

| Method | Plans | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Credit / Debit Card | All plans | Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover |
| ACH Bank Transfer | Professional, Enterprise | US only; 3–5 business day processing |
| SEPA Direct Debit | Professional, Enterprise | EU only |
| Wire Transfer | Enterprise only | Contact billing@example.com |
| Invoice (Net 30) | Enterprise only | Requires credit approval |

## Billing Cycle

- **Monthly billing**: Charged on the same date each month (e.g., subscribed on the 5th → billed on the 5th)
- **Annual billing**: Charged once per year; 20% discount applied
- Invoices are emailed to the billing email address and available in Billing → Invoice History

## Changing Your Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter new card details — they are securely tokenized via Stripe
4. Set as Default
5. Old payment method can be removed after setting the new default

## Understanding Your Invoice

- **Base plan fee**: Monthly or annual subscription cost
- **Overages**: Usage beyond your plan limits (seats, API calls, storage)
- **Taxes**: VAT or GST applied based on your billing country
- **Credits**: Discounts or SLA compensation credits applied to your balance

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
1. You receive an email notification immediately
2. We retry the charge after 3 days, then 7 days
3. After 3 failed attempts, your account is downgraded to the Free plan
4. Upgrade again after updating your payment method

## Requesting a Refund

Refunds are handled case-by-case by our billing team:
- Annual subscriptions: pro-rated refund within 14 days of renewal
- Monthly subscriptions: no refund for the current billing period
- Technical failures: full or partial refund may be issued

To request a refund, email billing@example.com with your invoice number and reason.

## Tax Exemption

If your organization is tax-exempt:
1. Email billing@example.com with your exemption certificate
2. We apply the exemption to future invoices within 2 business days

## Currency

All charges are processed in USD. For Enterprise customers, EUR and GBP billing are available on request.
