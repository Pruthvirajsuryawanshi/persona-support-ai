# Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to Our Platform

This guide walks you through the essential steps to get your account fully configured within the first 24 hours.

## Step 1: Account Verification

After sign-up, check your inbox for a verification email. Click the link within 15 minutes. If the link expires, go to **Settings → Account → Resend Verification**.

## Step 2: Profile Setup

Navigate to **Dashboard → Profile** and complete:
- Full name and company name
- Timezone (important for SLA calculations)
- Notification preferences (email / SMS / in-app)

## Step 3: Connect Your First Integration

Go to **Settings → Integrations** and connect at least one data source:
- CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Helpdesk (Zendesk, Freshdesk)
- Custom API (use your API key from Settings → API)

## Step 4: Invite Team Members

Go to **Settings → Team → Invite** to add colleagues. Assign roles:
- **Admin**: Full access including billing
- **Agent**: Can respond to tickets; no billing access
- **Viewer**: Read-only

## Step 5: Run Your First Test

Send a test message via **Dashboard → Sandbox** to verify that your integration pipeline is working end-to-end.

## Common Onboarding Issues

| Issue | Resolution |
|---|---|
| Verification email not received | Check spam folder; wait 5 min then resend |
| Integration fails to connect | Verify API key permissions; check firewall rules |
| Team invite bounced | Confirm the email domain is not blocked by your IT |

## Support

If you experience issues during onboarding, contact support@example.com with your Account ID.
